Title: Talking About Favorite Animals

Teacher: Great choice, Tommy! How about you, Sarah?
Sarah: I love horses. They're graceful, and I like riding them.
Teacher: Wonderful! Now, let's hear from Jack. What's your favorite animal?
Jack: I'm into birds, especially parrots. They can talk, and their colors are amazing.
Teacher: Interesting choice, Jack! Finally, Emily, what's your favorite animal?
Emily: I'm fascinated by dolphins. They're smart, and I love watching them swim.

Title: Exploring Favorite Hobbies - Short Chat with Four Students
Teacher: Hello again! Today, let's talk about our favorite hobbies. Tommy, what do you like to do in your free time?
Tommy: I enjoy drawing. It's fun to create new things on paper.
Teacher: That's great, Tommy! Sarah, how about you? Any favorite hobbies?
Sarah: I love playing soccer. It's exciting, and I get to run around with my friends.
Teacher: Awesome choice, Sarah! Jack, what's your favorite hobby?
Jack: I'm into reading. Books take me to different worlds, and I learn a lot from them.
Teacher: Fantastic, Jack! Now, Emily, what's your favorite hobby?
Emily: I enjoy gardening. Planting flowers and watching them grow is so peaceful.

Title: My Dream Vacation
Teacher: Hi everyone! Let's talk about our dream vacations. Tommy, where would you love to go for your dream vacation?
Tommy: I've always wanted to visit the Grand Canyon. The vast landscapes and deep canyons seem incredible.
Teacher: Wonderful choice, Tommy! Sarah, how about you? Any dream vacation in mind?
Sarah: I dream of going to Paris. The Eiffel Tower and the beautiful streets just sound magical.
Teacher: That sounds amazing, Sarah! Jack, where is your dream vacation destination?
Jack: I'd love to explore Japan. The mix of modern technology and traditional culture is fascinating.
Teacher: Great choice, Jack! Finally, Emily, where would you like to go for your dream vacation?
Emily: I dream of going to the Maldives. The crystal-clear waters and overwater bungalows look like paradise.
